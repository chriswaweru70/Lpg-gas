Why we stand out.

Keg Barrels

We also offer services in refurbishment of keg barrels. We repair and restore old or damaged barrels to look new again. Our team is proud of their production track record, in producing quality whilst meeting just in time deliveries.

Smart Meters

Our smart meters can digitally send meter readings to us for more accurate energy bills. They come with a smart energy monitor that shows you how much energy you are using in shillings and cents. Your transactions are simplified, you can make payments, check gas usage, receive invoices and much more, all with just a few clicks on your phone.
